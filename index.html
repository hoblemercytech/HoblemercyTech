FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before working together.

How long does a typical website project take?

A standard 5-page website takes 1–2 weeks. Full-stack web applications typically take 4–8 weeks depending on complexity. I'll give you a clear timeline estimate during our discovery call.

Do you work with international clients?

Yes! I work with clients worldwide. International projects are billed in USD and we communicate via WhatsApp, email, or Zoom. Timezone differences are never a barrier.

What do I need to provide to get started?

Just a clear idea of what you need and your budget range. I handle everything else — design, content structure, hosting setup, and deployment. You only need to provide your brand assets (logo, colors) if you already have them.

Do you offer payment in installments?

Yes. Standard projects are split 50% upfront and 50% on delivery. Larger enterprise projects can be split into 3 milestone payments. All payment terms are agreed before work begins.

Will I own the source code after the project?

Absolutely. Full source code is delivered to you on project completion. You own everything — the design, code, and all assets. No lock-in, no recurring licensing fees.

Can you maintain or update my site after launch?

Yes. All plans include 30–90 days of free post-launch support. After that, I offer affordable monthly retainer packages for ongoing updates, hosting management, and feature additions.

Do you build mobile apps as well?

Yes — React Native apps for both iOS and Android are available under the Professional and Enterprise plans. I can also convert existing web apps into Progressive Web Apps (PWA) for a lighter mobile experience.
